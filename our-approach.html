Your Personalized Care Pathway

We tailor every step of the process to your needs, from diagnosis to long-term support.

Initial Consultation

We’ll talk through your symptoms, review your medical history, and examine your airway in detail.

Sleep Testing

  • Home Sleep Apnea Test (HSAT): Convenient overnight test for most patients.
  • In-Lab Study: Used for complex or unclear cases requiring deeper analysis.
  • Prior Studies: If you've had a study within 12 months, we can often use it. If not, or if your weight/health has changed, we may recommend retesting.

Personalized Treatment

We work with you to choose the best option—CPAP, oral appliances, surgery, Inspire, or supportive care.

Follow-Up & Support

We're here for long-term guidance to ensure your treatment works and you feel better.
