Tailored Treatments for Better Sleep

There is no one-size-fits-all solution. We offer a range of personalized treatment options to address your unique needs and goals.

CPAP / BiPAP

Pressurized airflow therapy to keep your airway open during sleep. Considered first-line treatment for moderate to severe OSA.

Oral Appliances

Custom-fitted dental devices worn at night to gently reposition your jaw and improve airflow. Great for mild to moderate OSA or CPAP-intolerant patients.

Nasal & Sinus Surgery

Procedures like septoplasty, turbinate reduction, or balloon sinuplasty improve airflow through the nose, often improving CPAP tolerance and sleep quality.

Inspire Therapy

Implanted device that stimulates the airway to prevent collapse. For select patients with moderate to severe OSA who cannot tolerate CPAP.

Weight Management

We offer support through our in-house dietician and may recommend medications like Zepbound for eligible patients to support airway improvement through weight loss.

Allergy & Sinus Care

Comprehensive allergy testing and treatment, including immunotherapy, to reduce nasal inflammation and congestion that can worsen SDB.
