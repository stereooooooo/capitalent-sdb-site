Explore Your Treatment Options

We offer a full spectrum of sleep breathing therapies, tailored to your needs.

CPAP / BiPAP

Pressurized air therapy to keep your airway open during sleep.

Oral Appliances

Custom dental devices to treat snoring and mild to moderate OSA.

Nasal & Sinus Surgery

Procedures to improve nasal airflow and support better sleep.

Inspire Therapy

An implanted device to gently stimulate airway muscles while you sleep.

Weight Management

In-house nutrition and medical support for weight loss and sleep improvement.

Allergy & Sinus Care

Treatment for congestion and allergies that impact breathing.
