Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about sleep-disordered breathing diagnosis and treatment.

How long does it take to get used to CPAP?

Most patients adjust within a few weeks. We’ll help you troubleshoot along the way.

Will insurance cover treatment?

Yes—most plans cover sleep testing and CPAP. We’ll help you verify your benefits.

What if I can’t tolerate CPAP?

We offer alternatives like oral appliances, Inspire therapy, and nasal/throat surgery.

Do I need a new sleep study?

We review any recent studies. If more than a year old or your health has changed, we may recommend a new one.
