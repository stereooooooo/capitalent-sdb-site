Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common concerns about treatment, insurance, and care.

How long to adjust to CPAP?

Most patients adapt within 1–3 weeks with consistent use and good mask fit.

Is surgery the only option?

No. Oral appliances, Inspire, weight loss, and allergy care are all options.

Will insurance cover it?

We’ll help verify your benefits and guide you through your options.

How do I choose a treatment?

We’ll help you choose based on your study, anatomy, and preferences.
