Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about diagnosis, treatment, and support.

How long does it take to feel better after starting treatment?

Some patients feel better within days, while others may take several weeks. Sleep quality improves gradually, especially when therapy is consistent.

Do I have to use CPAP forever?

Not always. If your sleep apnea is mild or if weight loss or surgery improves your airway, you may be able to stop CPAP. However, many patients benefit from long-term use.

Will insurance cover Inspire therapy?

Most major insurers cover Inspire for eligible patients. We will work with your insurance and handle the approval process on your behalf.

Can I do telehealth visits?

Yes, we offer virtual follow-up visits when appropriate, especially for reviewing results, checking progress, or managing treatment plans.

What if I can’t tolerate CPAP?

There are many alternatives including oral appliances, Inspire therapy, nasal surgery, weight loss, and allergy treatment. We’ll help you find the right fit.
