Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get used to CPAP?

Most patients adapt within 1–3 weeks with the right mask and settings. We're here to help troubleshoot and encourage success.

Will insurance cover my sleep study and treatment?

Most insurance plans cover diagnostic sleep studies and CPAP therapy. We help verify your benefits and will explain any out-of-pocket costs before treatment.

Is surgery the only option if I can’t use CPAP?

No. We offer multiple alternatives including oral appliances, Inspire therapy, weight loss support, and in some cases, minimally invasive procedures.

How do I know which treatment is right for me?

We guide you based on your sleep study, anatomy, symptoms, lifestyle, and preferences. The best treatment is the one that works for you and fits your needs.
