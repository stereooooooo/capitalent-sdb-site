Your Personalized Care Pathway

We believe in a thorough, individualized approach to help you achieve restful sleep and better health.

What Was Covered at Your Initial Visit

During your first appointment, we performed a comprehensive evaluation to better understand your sleep concerns. This included:

  • Medical history review – focusing on sleep symptoms, lifestyle factors, and related conditions
  • Physical exam – with attention to your nose, throat, and airway anatomy
  • Nasal endoscopy – if needed, to directly visualize internal nasal structures
  • Sinus CT scan – if sinus disease or nasal blockage was suspected

Based on this evaluation, we’ve recommended a personalized care plan which may include sleep testing, treatment options, and follow-up steps to help improve your sleep and long-term health.

Sleep Testing: Home vs. Lab-Based

Home Sleep Apnea Test (HSAT)

  • Convenient and done in your own bed
  • Recommended for patients with high likelihood of moderate-severe OSA
  • Measures breathing, oxygen levels, respiratory effort
  • Not ideal if insomnia, limb movement issues, or other disorders suspected
  • Usually lower cost

In-Lab Sleep Study (Polysomnography / PSG)

  • Performed overnight in a sleep center
  • Recommended if HSAT is inconclusive or for patients with complex sleep concerns
  • Measures brainwaves, eye movement, muscle activity, heart rhythm, and more
  • Best for suspected central sleep apnea, parasomnias, or comorbid insomnia
  • More comprehensive, but higher cost and less convenient

Choosing a Treatment Plan

We take a personalized approach to treatment. Your plan is based on factors including the severity of sleep apnea, anatomy, comorbid conditions, lifestyle, and your treatment preferences. We review all suitable options, from CPAP and oral appliances to surgery, Inspire therapy, and weight loss support. Our goal is to help you find a long-term solution that fits your needs and improves your quality of life.
