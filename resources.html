Helpful Tips for Treatment Success

Guidance on managing CPAP, weight loss, allergies, and more.

CPAP Tips

  • Focus on fit and comfort
  • Use humidification
  • Ease into use gradually
  • Follow up with your DME provider

Allergies & Sleep

Nasal congestion can worsen sleep issues. Treatments help improve airflow and rest.

Weight Loss & Sleep

We offer support for weight loss to help reduce or resolve sleep-disordered breathing.

Your Role in Treatment

  • Stick to the plan
  • Communicate challenges
  • Be persistent
