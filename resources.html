CPAP Success Tips

  • Ensure your mask fits comfortably
  • Use humidification to reduce dryness
  • Ease into usage by wearing it while reading or relaxing
  • Keep your equipment clean
  • Track your nightly usage and follow up with your DME provider

Allergies & Sleep

Allergic nasal congestion can make SDB worse. Treatments like nasal sprays, antihistamines, or allergy immunotherapy can help improve breathing and sleep quality.

Weight Loss & Sleep

Losing weight can reduce the severity of OSA. We offer in-house nutrition counseling and can prescribe medications when appropriate to support long-term success.
