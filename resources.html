Helpful Resources

Tips, tools, and educational content to support your success with sleep-disordered breathing treatment.

CPAP Success Tips

  • Use a well-fitted mask
  • Start with short, daytime wear sessions
  • Clean equipment regularly
  • Use humidification if needed
  • Talk to your DME provider if you’re struggling

Allergies & Sleep

Allergies and congestion can worsen SDB. We offer allergy testing, immunotherapy, and sinus treatment to help.

Weight Loss & Sleep Apnea

Weight loss can reduce or eliminate SDB symptoms. We provide in-house nutrition services and medication support when appropriate.
